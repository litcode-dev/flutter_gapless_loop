## 0.0.1

* Initial release.
* Sample-accurate gapless looping on iOS via AVAudioEngine and Android via AudioTrack.
* Configurable loop region (start/end in seconds).
* Optional crossfade between loop iterations.
* Volume control and seek support.
* `stateStream`, `errorStream`, and `routeChangeStream` for reactive UI integration.
* Audio route change events (e.g. headphones unplugged).
